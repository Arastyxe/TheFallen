The Fallen

The fallen shall rise once more

About

A knight who attempts to flee during battle is struck down, and awakens in purgatory for the dishonored, otherwise known as the Land of the Fallen. A Mysterious Statue offers him a chance to reclaim his honour and gain access to eternal rest, only if he can seek out and slay the three Fallen Guardians and place their helms upon the Statue’s pedestal.

The Crypt

• Travel to the Crypt to find the Dybbuk - Possessor of Souls, a hateful spirit in possession of a grand soldier’s body.

The Swamp

• Travel to the Swamp to seek out the Behemoth - Devourer of the Dead, a Gargantuan who feasts on the rotting flesh of the dead.

The Cathedral

• Travel to the Cathedral to defeat Azriel - The Angel of Death, A former knight who rejected the path to eternal rest, and instead stands as the last Guardian to any knight who deems themselves worthy of redemption
